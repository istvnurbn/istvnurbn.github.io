Recently I've received a notification from my hosting provider that the yearly installment is due for payment on the 20th of December. This fact got me thinking, so here is the deal.

<!--more-->
 
Honestly, I'm not achieving what I intended to do when I started this website. I had vivid dreams about posting at least biweekly and how much it will help me to articulate my thoughts. There were times when I enjoyed writing posts but other times it was quite the opposite. It felt like a burden. There were no KPIs attached to this but still, I am not that satisfied.
 
The above described is a common theme with me recently. I do not feel successful or do not feel supported in the things I do. So, I have the following options to go forward:
 
1. Continue to have this site as-is without expectations from myself.
2. Change things up and relaunch the site under a new moniker and different tooling. (I came to dislike the Vox Scurra name anyway :sweat_smile:)
3. Write this blog to me in Markdown files without ever publishing them.
4. Stop doing all of this.
 
I'm leaning toward option two. I enjoy tinkering with the underlying system, sometimes more than writing posts. I also think that new things tend to change rapidly in the beginning. This website is no exception either.
